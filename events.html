CoderPassion and Pixel Paradise Hackathon

Dates & Times:

September 23rd and 24th, 8am-12pm at Pixel Paradise in Lexington MA.

Description:

Our Hackathon is a two-day event filled with coding, games, and teamwork. Kids will come together to learn about programming, create their own games, and solve fun challenges. You'll work with a team to think of cool ideas and then turn them into a real project. Along the way, helpful volunteers will be there to guide you, and you'll even get to enjoy lunch and win prizes. Whether you're new to coding or already a young expert, this hackathon is a chance to learn, make friends, and have a great time! Grab your spot now and get ready for two days filled with coding, creativity, teamwork, and lots of fun. Hurry, we only have room for 50 students from 5th to 12th grade!

October Month Long Coding Challenge

Dates & Times:

October 1st to October 31st, online.

Description:

This October, spark your child's creativity with our unique Coding Challenge! Throughout the month, three coding puzzles will be released every week, ranging from easy to hard, ensuring a fun learning experience for all skill levels. Your child can join with up to three friends to form a team, tackling the challenges and submitting their solutions through a detailed and guided process. They'll have a blast competing with other teams on our online leaderboard, earning points for each challenge they conquer. Plus, there's a fantastic prize awaiting the winning team, making this event not just a great way to learn and collaborate, but a thrilling competition they won't want to miss!

Sign Ups:

Sign ups will officially open on September 20th.

Student Project Showcase

Dates & Times:

In the week of November 5th-12th, official date is TBD. Will be hosted in Lexington.

Description:

The Project Showcase challenge is a special event that reflects what we truly believe in: using the skills your children learn to build something amazing! During the last three weeks of our curriculum, we guide students in crafting their own projects, making sure they have the support they need to bring their creative ideas to life. To celebrate the hard work and to keep our community strong, we're hosting this joyful event for all our students and their families right here in Lexington. At the event, your child will have 5 minutes to proudly present their project to everyone, with live demos to show off what they've built. It's an opportunity for your children to shine, share their achievements, and enjoy a memorable day with family and friends. Don't let them miss this exciting celebration of creativity and learning!

Sign Ups:

Sign ups will officially open on November 1st.
